# Species Data Sources

## Official Sources

### 1. IUCN Red List
**URL:** https://www.iucnredlist.org

**Search Parameters:**
- Geographic range: Indonesia
- Taxonomic groups: Mammals, Birds, Plants, Reptiles, Amphibians
- Red List Category: Critically Endangered (CR), Endangered (EN), Vulnerable (VU)

**Data Available:**
- Conservation status (assessed 2020-2024)
- Population trends
- Geographic distribution
- Threats classification

### 2. Kementerian Lingkungan Hidup dan Kehutanan (KLHK)
**URL:** https://www.menlhk.go.id

**Relevant Documents:**
- Peraturan Menteri LHK No. P.106/MENLHK/SETJEN/KUM.1/12/2018 tentang Jenis Tumbuhan dan Satwa yang Dilindungi
- Daftar Spesies Prioritas untuk Konservasi Indonesia 2020-2025
- Statistik Keanekaragaman Hayati Indonesia 2023

### 3. CITES (Convention on International Trade in Endangered Species)
**URL:** https://cites.org/eng/resources/species.html

**Search:** Indonesia native species in Appendix I and II

### 4. LIPI (Lembaga Ilmu Pengetahuan Indonesia)
**URL:** https://www.lipi.go.id

**Relevant Research:**
- Katalog Flora Indonesia
- Katalog Fauna Indonesia
- Indonesian Biodiversity Journal

### 5. BirdLife International Data Zone
**URL:** https://datazone.birdlife.org/country/indonesia

**Focus:** Endemic bird species and Important Bird Areas (IBAs)

## Data Verification Checklist

### Conservation Status
- [ ] Status IUCN sesuai dengan assessment terbaru (2020-2024)
- [ ] Kode status: CR (Critically Endangered), EN (Endangered), VU (Vulnerable)
- [ ] Trend populasi: Decreasing/Stable/Increasing

### Taxonomy
- [ ] Nama latin sesuai dengan Catálogo da Vida / ITIS / CoL
- [ ] Nama umum Indonesia standar
- [ ] Taksonomi terbaru (2020-2024)

### Distribution
- [ ] Provinsi sesuai data administrasi Indonesia (UU No. 11 Tahun 2022)
- [ ] Koordinat geografis (opsional untuk verifikasi)
- [ ] Ekosistem/habitat spesifik

### Images
- [ ] Wikimedia Commons (CC BY-SA, CC BY, or Public Domain)
- [ ] Arkive (EOL - Encyclopedia of Life)
- [ ] iNaturalist (CC licensed)
- [ ] Kredit fotografer jelas

### Population Data
- [ ] Estimasi dengan tahun data
- [ ] Sumber publikasi ilmiah
- [ ] Metodologi survey (jika tersedia)
